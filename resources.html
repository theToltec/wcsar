Donate Today!

Washoe County Search & Rescue is a 501(c)(3) organization. We receive no tax dollars and do not charge for our services. We depend on the generosity of you the public and our members to help us with our financial obligations.

Your donations, grants and bequests enable us to save countless lives each year. In addition, we save the tax payers hundreds of thousands of dollars each year by Volunteering our time, equipment, and vehicles.

Please consider helping with a donation today!
